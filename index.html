About

Tom is an experienced software engineer who is passionate about building maintainable, extensible systems, providing a friendly face to engineering, and building lasting culture.

With years of experience spanning multiple technologies, teams large and small, and a variety of domains, Tom has packed a surprising amount into his short career. He packed a 4 year Bachelor's in Computer Science degree into 2 years while working full-time as a developer, and hasn't slowed down since.

Tom is a competent developer who is zealous about engineering culture. If you're interested in discussing his thoughts on building a strong organization or architecting lasting code, please send him a tweet or email.
